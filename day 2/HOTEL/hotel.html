ABOUT US

Express Inn is a family of distinctive hotels and resorts across the nation. From bustling cityscapes to serene landscapes, we believe every journey deserves a sanctuary of comfort, personalized service, and memorable moments. Founded in 2004 with a passion for hospitality and a deep appreciation for cultural diversity, we’ve grown into a renowned chain catering to a discerning clientele. In 2023 we made a progression by launching a new brand FREESIA. Our habitats under Freesia cater to leisure travellers as well as the corporate sector. By blending contemporary design with unique architectural elements and rich narratives of the place, each of our properties embodies the local spirit and culture. At Express Inn we don’t just offer rooms; we offer gateways to discovery. Our dedicated team curates authentic experiences be it a wedding a conference or a corporate offsite. Today, our hotels grace iconic landscapes, each one reflecting the unique character of its locale. We are committed to sustainability, minimizing our environmental impact and supporting local communities as we understand the importance of preserving the beauty of the world for future generations.
